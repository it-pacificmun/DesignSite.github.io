PacificMUN is an annual Model United Nations Conference that takes place At the Pinnacle Harbourfront Hotel in downtown Vancouver on February 23rd to 25th. The conference starts on Friday afternoon at 3:30, and will run until the closing ceremonies on Sunday afternoon. Between those times, PacificMUN is a venue for new experiences, passionate debate, and valuable socializing.
